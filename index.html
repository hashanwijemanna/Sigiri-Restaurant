Welcome to Sigiri Restaurant, an exquisite culinary haven nestled near the iconic Sigiriya Rock in the heart of Sri Lanka!

Our restaurant is a celebration of the rich cultural tapestry of this beautiful island, offering a dining experience that is as unique and captivating as the historic landmark that graces our surroundings.

At Sigiri Restaurant, we take pride in curating a menu that reflects the diverse flavors and traditions of Sri Lankan cuisine. Our chefs skillfully blend the aromatic spices and fresh, locally-sourced ingredients to create a symphony of tastes that transport you to the heart of this vibrant island.

Immerse yourself in the warm and inviting ambiance of our restaurant, where traditional elements seamlessly blend with modern design. Whether you choose to dine indoors surrounded by tasteful decor or prefer the open-air experience with views of Sigiriya Rock in the distance, every corner of our space is designed to enhance your dining pleasure.
